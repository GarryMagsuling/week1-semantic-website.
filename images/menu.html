The Menu

Here are our standard daily dishes.

Classic Munggo

Ingredients: Traditional savory mung bean stew cooked with garlic, onions, and tomatoes, enriched with pork pieces and topped with fresh green leaves. Best paired with steamed rice.

Price: ₱20.00

Classic Pork Adobo

Ingredients: Tender pork chunks braised in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and whole peppercorns, served with steamed white rice.

Price: ₱50.10

Sinigang na Manok

Ingredients: Tender pieces of chicken simmered in a sour, savory tamarind broth loaded with radish, sitaw (long beans), eggplant, and fresh leafy greens.

Price: ₱25.50

Beef Kare-Kare

Ingredients: Slow-cooked beef portions in a thick, rich peanut sauce, served with local vegetables and savory bagoong (shrimp paste) on the side.

Price: ₱200.00

Sizzling Sisig Tacos

Ingredients: Three soft corn tortillas filled with crispy pork sisig, onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of calamansi.

Price: ₱35.00
